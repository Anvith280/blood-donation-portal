Become a Voluntary Donor

Register your profile as a blood or plasma donor. Patients in your city will be able to find and contact you directly.

Your registration takes less than 2 minutes. Once done, your profile is listed in our directory and visible to anyone searching for donors in your city.

🩸

Blood Donation Eligibility

You should be 18–65 years old, weigh at least 45 kg, and be in good general health. Blood can be donated once every 90 days.

🧪

COVID-19 Plasma Donors

If you've recovered from COVID-19, you may be eligible to donate convalescent plasma to help critically ill patients. Donation is typically possible 28 days after recovery.

🔒

Your Privacy Matters

Only your name, blood group, city, and contact info is shown. You always have the choice whether to donate — this is completely voluntary.

✅

Free & Always Will Be

RaktSetu is a free public service. We do not charge donors or recipients. Blood donation must always be voluntary and unpaid.
